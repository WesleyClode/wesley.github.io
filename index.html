About Me

Currently I am a graduate student of School of Information Science and Technology and a member of VISeer Group, led by professor Quan Li.

Education

  • M.S. student: Computer Science and Technology, School of Information Science and Technology, ShanghaiTech University, China

  • B.Sc. degree: Astronomy, School of Astronomy and Space Science, Nanjing University, China.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in Data Visualization and Visual Analytics, promoting the interaction and influence of human, computer, and data, and boosting data analysis and mining efficiency that involves large-scale data, complex applications and models, forming “human-in-the-loop” analysis.

Now I mainly focus on the application of VIS in certain fields, such as the visual analysis of federated learning and the visual analysis of the recommendation system based on graph neural network.
